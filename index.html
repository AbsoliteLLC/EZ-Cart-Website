🛒 What is EZ Cart?

Have you ever wanted to watch the price of an iPad on Best Buy, a monitor on Amazon, and a jacket on Walmart, all in one centralized place?

EZ Cart is a lightweight, on-demand Chrome Extension that acts as your personal, automated shopping list. Instead of keeping 20 tabs open or creating accounts on multiple price-tracking websites, simply save items directly to your browser. Whenever you open the extension, it instantly fetches the live, real-time prices for every item on your list.
