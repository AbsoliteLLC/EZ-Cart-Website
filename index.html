🛒 What is EZ Cart?

Have you ever wanted to watch the price of an iPad on Best Buy, a monitor on Amazon, and a jacket on Walmart, all in one place?

EZ Cart is a lightweight Chrome Extension that acts as your personal, automated shopping list. Instead of keeping 20 tabs open, simply save the item to your tracker. Whenever you open the extension, it instantly fetches the live, real-time prices for every item on your list.

⚙️ How It Works

1. Save Anything

Browsing a store? Click "Add to Tracker" to save the product and its current price.

2. On-Demand Refresh

Open the extension anytime to trigger a live price check. Watch your list update with the newest prices in milliseconds.

3. Zero-Cloud Privacy

Your list lives purely in your browser's local storage. No servers, no tracking your browsing history, and no account required.

4. Buy When Ready

See a price drop? Click the "Buy Now" button to be taken directly to the retailer's checkout page.

🔒 Privacy First

Unlike other trackers that monitor every website you visit, EZ Cart only activates when you physically click the extension icon. Your data stays on your hard drive, exactly where it belongs.
