🛒 What is EZ Cart?

Have you ever wanted to watch the price of an iPad, a monitor, and a jacket all in one centralized place?

EZ Cart is a lightweight, on-demand Chrome Extension that acts as your personal, automated shopping list. Instead of keeping 20 tabs open or creating accounts on multiple price-tracking websites, simply save items directly to your browser. Whenever you open the extension, it instantly fetches the live, real-time prices for every item on your list.

⚙️ Key Features

1. Save Anything, Anywhere

Browsing a store? Click "+ Add Current Item" to instantly save the product, its title, its image, and its current price.

2. On-Demand Price Refresh

Open the extension anytime and click "Update" to trigger a live price check. Watch your list update with the newest prices in milliseconds.

3. Beautiful Dark Mode

Enjoy a sleek, modern interface with a built-in Dark Mode that perfectly organizes your items into clean product cards.

4. Buy When Ready

See a price drop? Click the "Buy Now" button to be taken directly to the retailer's checkout page.

🔒 Privacy First: Zero-Cloud Tracking

We believe your shopping habits are your business. Unlike other trackers that monitor every website you visit, EZ Cart only activates when you physically click the extension icon. We do not require a login, we do not sync your data to the cloud, and we do not track your background web activity. Your data stays on your hard drive.
